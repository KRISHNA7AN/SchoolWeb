Campus Life

School Library

Was Established in 2018

This Is the New school library which opened in 2018 and we also had a old library which has now turned into the year 2 classrooms. In the library the are loads of books, 2 reading corners, chess boards and tables with paper and pens to draw. This is our newest building at the school.

Sports Hall

Unsure When Established

This is the sports hall and is used for lots of things. The Multipurpose Sports Hall suits a variety of sports including Basketball, Futsal, Dodge ball, Volleyball and others. It has tiered seating for up to 100 spectators and is in close proximity to our large car park. The hall is fully air-conditioned and includes kitchen and canteen facilities. There are change rooms and amenities for home and away teams, as well as wheelchair access and amenities. The hall also has a sound system and public address system. It is Also used for Assemblies.
